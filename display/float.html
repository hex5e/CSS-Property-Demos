This paragraph is below the cleared float.

The float property is used to position elements to the left or right, allowing text and inline content to wrap around them. The clear property is used to prevent content from flowing around floated elements.
Floats are less common in modern layouts (use flex or grid instead), but are still useful for certain effects.
