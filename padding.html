Demonstrating padding

The dashed line is the element’s border; the space between that line and the text is the element’s padding.

Same padding on all sides

padding: 0;
padding: 10px;
padding: 40px;

Different horizontal / vertical padding

padding: 10px 5px;
padding: 5px 20px;

Each side explicitly

padding: 5px 20px 40px 60px;
(T R B L)

Resize the window or inspect the elements to see how the padding stays inside the border, pushing the content inward without affecting neighboring elements (that’s what margin is for).
